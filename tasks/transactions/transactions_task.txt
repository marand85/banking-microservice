# Transactions

A large bank processes thousands of transactions on its accounts every day.
These include both incoming transfers (credits) and outgoing transfers (debits).

One department wants to know how the balance on every account changes after all daily transactions are processed.
They also require the account list to be sorted in ascending order.

It is expected that in the near future the number of transactions could reach 100,000 as the customer base keeps growing.
The system must be ready for that!

## Task
Write an algorithm that processes all transactions and returns a sorted list of accounts, including the number of credits, debits, and the final balance.
The starting balance of every account is assumed to be 0.
